How it works

From rubric to results
in three steps

No training required. If you can attach a file, you can use Markulate.

01
Upload your rubric
Paste it in or upload a PDF. Markulate learns from your criteria exactly as written — your standards, not ours.
02
Submit the scripts
Upload student responses one by one or as a batch. Typed or scanned. Markulate handles both.
03
Review, adjust, export

Student data protection

How Markulate protects
your students' work

We know you're uploading real student work. Here is exactly how we handle it.

🔒
Your data is yours
Student scripts and rubrics you upload belong to you and your institution. We do not sell, share, or transfer them to any third party.
🚫
Not used for model training
Student work uploaded to Markulate is never used to train AI models — ours or anyone else's. What your students write stays private.
📋
Full audit trail
Every mark, every override, every rationale is logged and exportable. You always have a clear, defensible record of every grading decision.
👤
Teacher-approved always
No mark is ever issued without your review and approval. Markulate is a first-pass assistant — final authority stays with you, always.
